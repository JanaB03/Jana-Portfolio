Project Overview

Social Context

High school and early college students in the stages of career exploration who are uncertain about their path due to unfamiliarity with different professions.

Our Solution

A VR-powered platform that combines personalized career matching, immersive job shadowing, and meaningful connections with industry professionals.

Expected Impact

Help students make more informed career decisions through hands-on experience and direct interaction with professionals in their field of interest.

Research & Discovery

Methodology

  • 4 semi-structured interviews
  • 2 focus group sessions
  • Competitive analysis
  • User journey mapping

Key Findings

  • Career fairs are overwhelming and disorganized
  • Students value in-person interaction
  • Professionals want to connect with motivated students

First Iteration

Initial Features:

  • Career quiz matching
  • Independent VR demo videos
  • Slack integration for communication
  • Basic aptitude tests

Key Learnings:

  • VR sessions needed more interaction
  • Popular careers created bottlenecks
  • Communication platform needed better integration

Prototype 2 - Refined Flow

1. Enhanced Career Quiz

Improved matching algorithm with more relevant options

2. Social Integration

Join Slack channels for peer and professional connection

3. Interactive VR Experience

Complete VR walkthrough with recruiter guidance

4. Rotation & Networking

Explore second career match with same format

5. Follow-up

Recruiters connect with top candidates
