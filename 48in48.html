Chapter 1

The 48-Hour Challenge

48in48 pairs non-profits with volunteer designers & developers to build production-ready websites over a weekend. Our client—the local farmers market—needed donation, volunteer, vendor and schedule features fast.

“We need a site the community can trust—by Sunday night.”

— Kick-off, Friday 6 PM

We set one goal: ship the highest-impact MVP without sacrificing accessibility or brand.

Chapter 2

About Our Client

Meet Judy

Our client is the visionary behind sustainable farmers markets in our community. Her mission is to create vibrant spaces where local vendors and shoppers connect while promoting environmental consciousness.

Through her innovative approach, she has launched multiple market locations, empowering over 100+ local vendors and serving thousands of community members.

Our client, who manages the local farmers market, came to us with specific goals for a new digital presence: make it easy to donate, recruit and schedule volunteers, bring in more vendors, showcase market programs and schedules, and highlight community initiatives. These goals shaped every design and development decision during the 48-hour sprint.

Chapter 2

Project Objectives (from kickoff)

Since this was a ground-up build, we aligned on clear outcomes with the client.

Make donating effortless

Prominent giving paths, simple form, and clear impact messaging.

  • One-step donation flow
  • Option for recurring gifts
  • Automated receipts

Recruit and schedule volunteers

Shift-first sign-up that confirms instantly and reduces coordinator workload.

  • Shift picker with confirmations
  • Cleaner intake data
  • Automatic reminders (post-MVP)

Simplify vendor onboarding

One page to understand criteria, submit documents, and track status.

  • Requirements & fees up front
  • Single application flow
  • Status clarity for farmers
