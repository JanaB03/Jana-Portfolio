Connecting Care Providers With Those In Need

CommuniCare Connect is a mobile application designed to bridge communication gaps and improve service coordination for homeless individuals and care providers.

Explore Features

Key Features

CommuniCare Connect brings together essential tools in one platform to streamline outreach and support services.

Check-in System

Simple and quick attendance tracking at shelters, food services, and other facilities to monitor client engagement and service usage.

Secure Messaging

Direct communication channel between clients and case managers, ensuring timely support and reducing barriers to accessing assistance.

Location Sharing

Optional real-time location sharing to help case managers locate clients in need and coordinate outreach services more effectively.

Resource Directory

Comprehensive database of local shelters, food services, healthcare facilities, and more, with filtering options and real-time availability updates.

Mobile Clinic Tracker

Real-time updates on mobile health clinic locations, schedules, and services to improve access to healthcare for vulnerable populations.

Analytics Dashboard

Data visualization tools for organizations to track service usage, identify trends, and improve resource allocation based on real needs.

Design Process

My approach to designing CommuniCare Connect focused on understanding both the needs of homeless individuals and care providers.

1

Research & Discovery

I conducted interviews with case managers, shelter staff, healthcare providers, and individuals experiencing homelessness to understand pain points and needs. Key findings revealed communication gaps, difficulty tracking client locations, and challenges in coordinating services.

2

Information Architecture

Based on research insights, I developed a user-centered information architecture that prioritizes quick access to essential features. The dual-interface approach caters to both client users and service providers with tailored experiences.

3

Wireframing & Prototyping

I created low-fidelity wireframes to establish core functionality and navigation patterns. These were refined through multiple iterations and developed into interactive prototypes for user testing.

4

User Testing

Prototype testing was conducted with both target user groups. Feedback was collected on usability, accessibility, and feature relevance. Key improvements included simplifying the check-in process and enhancing map features for location sharing.

5

Visual Design & UI Development

I developed a clean, accessible visual design system with a focus on clarity and ease of use. The color palette uses contrasting colors to help users quickly identify different features and actions, with considerations for visibility in various lighting conditions.
